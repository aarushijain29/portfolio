University of Waterloo

In recognition of outstanding academic achievement in an academic term, the designation "Term Dean's Honours List" is awarded to undergraduate Mathematics students who satisfy all of the following criteria for the term:

    1. Registered in an honours plan with a Term Average (TAV) of at least 87%.
    2. Normally enrolled in at least 2.5 units of courses with numeric or letter grades.
